Tropical retreat in Mooloolah Valley

219 Neill Road, Mooloolah Valley
Privately positioned on 1.64 acres of rolling lawns and native and tropical rainforest, this unique three-bedroom residence exudes warmth and character throughout and boasts a vast outdoor entertaining area and wrap-around verandahs overlooking the established gardens and inground swimming pool. Featuring an open-plan kitchen, dining and living area with high vaulted ceilings, a cosy wood burning stove and light filled interiors this home is a country retreat only three minutes to the vibrant town Mooloolah Valley.

- Modern kitchen overlooking dining/living room
- Master, built in wardrobes, opens onto verandah with leafy backdrop
- Two further bedrooms, one with walk in robe, ceiling fans
- Airconditioning, additional family room/office
- Stunning pool, fire pit and expansive entertaining areas
- Spacious family bathroom
- 5.45m x 9.25m storage for the caravan or boat
- 6m x 9m shed with workshop
- Bore and grey water septic system
- Three minute drive to Mooloolah town, train station, cafes, shops, school
- 25 minutes to Sunshine Coast beaches
